Chart










This graph shows the number of SoCs made by companies among top performing devices. SoCs stand for 'system-ona-chip' and it pretty much represents the brain of our smartphones. An SoC contains multiple processing parts, memory, modems, and other essential bits and pieces manufactured together in a single chip that is soldered onto the circuit board. In this graph you can see that qualcomm has created more SoCs than all other companies, and that is because other companies use qualcomm's SoCs.
